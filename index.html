About me

For more then a five years of my personal development I've been building quietly on the principles that got me started – simplicity, hard working and having fun. I am passionate about the web. I practise a modern web design characterized by minimalism. I have strong belife in creative web design and development. I see great impact of the web and how it can change the world - the way we think, the way we express ideas, we collaborate, we connect with information and we comunicate with each other. It show me all possibilities to improve my skills.
